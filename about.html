About Me

I was born on the beautiful Isle of Wight, raised in the Cotswolds, and have spent the last five years surrounded by the Cornish Coastline, so a love for the natural world is a fundamental part of who I am. Many of my hobbies and interests revolve around wildlife and the outdoors; I love to hike, and even studied Marine and Natural History Photography at University, which fully immersed me in my love for nature. However, I am also fascinated by the more scientific side to the natural world, and enjoy learning about the logical patterns in animal and plant behaviour.

My interest in science reflects my other main passion, which is technology. The wonders of where tech will take us next never ceases to amaze me, and it is this admiration which brought me to my passion for coding. I love the logical structure and problem-solving associated with coding, where I can stretch my creative legs whilst using patience and determination to overcome complex challenges. In my free time, I can usually be found adding to my collection of projects, including website design and game design. The feeling of creating something from nothing always gives me a fantastic sense of achievement, so I’m always looking out for my next challenge and inspiration.

Now I’m entering the next chapter of my life, I’m ever excited to see where my passions for nature and technology take me next.
